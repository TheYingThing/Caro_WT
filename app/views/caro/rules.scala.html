Welcome to caro

Caro is a multiplayer strategy board game. This implementation allows for two players.

How to play

The rules and point-distribution are explained in the following section

Rules

The following rules are applied:

  • Same color tiles may not be placed next to each other
  • No more than three tiles of the same color may be placed diagonally
  • No more than two neighboring tiles may be of the same color
  • Placed tiles may not exceed 6x6 in dimensions

Points

The following tile combinations yield the following points:

: 10 points
: 8 points
: 6 points
: 4 points
: 2 points
: 1 points

Furthermore, the following circumstances grant special points:

  • 10 points will be granted when placing the first tile of the game
  • 10 points will be subtracted upon breaking a rule
  • Double points will be granted when a player places the last tile of one of their colors
